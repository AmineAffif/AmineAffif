# Hey, I'm Amine

A passionate Fullstack Developer from France, ready for a new challenge. I love building software that combines efficiency, creativity, and real-world impact. My background spans web/mobile dev, UX/UI, and even a bit of marketing and automation.

## Experiences

- **Développeur Web/Mobile (Agency) – Stage @ GYRA (2021)**  
  Conception and deployment of React Native mobile apps and business-oriented web platforms (React, Laravel). Client-facing technical estimates, time management across multiple projects, and multi-team coordination.

- **Fullstack Developer (Callcenter 2.0) – Alternance @ StudyCall (2023)**  
  Rails-based web solutions, AI integrations, optimizing infrastructure costs. Automated and simplified user flows, incident management, and training newcomers on technical processes.

- **Fullstack Developer (Opticien 2.0) – Alternance @ Cévidentia (2024)**  
  Ruby on Rails and Next.js maintenance and new features, adapting to business growth, delivering high-performance platforms. Agile methods, CI/CD pipelines on GitHub, pairing with senior devs for advanced design patterns.

- **Fullstack Developer (EdTech) – Alternance @ L’Étudiant - StudyAdvisor (2022)**  
  Dynamic solutions with Rails and React Native, meeting business requirements and user immersion sessions. Coordinated across teams, used agile to deliver quickly, led technical and functional feedback sessions.

- **Web Developer ❤️ Freelance @ DreamLocation (2025)**  
  Complete web platform for a car rental company. Stripe payment integration, real-time booking system, cron-based automation, GDPR compliance, admin dashboards, email campaigns, design direction with Photoshop, and close client collaboration.

- **Web/Graphic Developer ❤️ Freelance @ Evalawrens (2023)**  
  Brand creation for face-cleansing brushes from concept to marketing. Unique visual identity, professional ad videos with After Effects, WooCommerce site with CRM automation, marketing campaigns on social platforms, achieving break-even with performance optimization and bold design.
